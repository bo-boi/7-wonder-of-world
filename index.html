Seven Wonders of World 


New 7 Wonders of the World was a campaign started in 2000 to choose Wonders 
of the World from a selection of 200 existing monuments. The popularity poll
via free Web-based voting and small amounts of telephone voting was led by 
Canadian-Swiss Bernard Weber and organized by the New 7 Wonders Foundation
(N7W) based in Zurich, Switzerland, with winners announced on 7 July 2007
in Lisbon, at Estádio da Luz.
